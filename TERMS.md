## 1. What you are agreeing to

By using Zunga you accept these terms. Zunga is a free utility that prepares and runs the USSD codes you would otherwise dial yourself, on your own SIM.

## 2. Zunga is not a financial institution

- Zunga does **not** hold, transfer, or process money.
- Every payment happens inside your carrier's or bank's own USSD session, authorized by **your** PIN typed into **their** popup.
- All transfer fees shown or charged belong to your carrier, bank, or eKash (the national rail) — Zunga adds nothing and receives nothing from your transactions.

## 3. Your responsibilities

- Confirm the recipient in your network's own confirmation step before entering your PIN. The registered name your network shows is the authoritative check.
- Keep your carrier PIN secret. Zunga will never ask for it — anyone who does is not Zunga.
- Use codes only on a SIM registered to you.

## 4. Accuracy of codes

USSD codes are published by carriers and banks and can change without notice. Zunga ships them as remotely-updatable signed data and corrects them as fast as possible, but your carrier's session is always the source of truth. If a code misroutes, cancel in the carrier popup — nothing is sent without your PIN.
